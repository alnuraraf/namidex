Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Nami — a passionate developer and animation enthusiast. I created NamiTube to share the joy of anime and animated films with fans around the world.

Mission

To provide a free, premium streaming experience for animation lovers. No ads, no clutter — just great content, beautifully presented.

About the Project

NamiTube is built on the TMDB API and designed with a dark, cinematic aesthetic. Every line of code is crafted to feel premium and immersive.

Our Vision

NamiTube is more than just a streaming site — it's a celebration of animation. From timeless classics to the latest anime, every title is handpicked to ensure a world-class experience. We believe that great stories deserve a great home, and that's what we've built.

Always free. Always animation.
