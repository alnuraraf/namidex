Frequently asked

Everything you might want to know before donating.

Where does the money go?

Server hosting, CDN bandwidth, domain renewals, and a small reserve for emergency infrastructure. We publish a yearly transparency report on the Discord server.

Do donors get extra anime / exclusive episodes?

No. All catalog content stays free for everyone — anime data comes from AniList and streams come from third-party servers we don't control. Donor perks are limited to community recognition (badges, channels, early changelog access).

Are crypto donations refundable?

On-chain transactions are final and non-refundable. Please double-check the address and network before sending.

Can I cancel a recurring donation?

Yes, anytime — from your Ko-fi or Patreon dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Do you store any personal data when I donate?

Donations are processed by the payment providers (Ko-fi, Patreon, PayPal) or directly on-chain. We don't run our own accounts system and never see your card or wallet credentials.
